# Yoma SMS Signup Guide

This guide explains how users get registered on Yoma through our SMS-integrated signup system.

## How It Works

1. **Registration via Advanta**:
   - You provide your information (name, phone number, etc.) to Advanta
   - Advanta securely sends this information to our system
   - Our system registers you with Yoma

2. **Account Creation**:
   - Your account is created on Yoma with the phone number you provided
   - A temporary password is automatically generated

3. **First Login**:
   - Go to the Yoma website: https://yoma.world
   - Enter your phone number
   - You'll receive an OTP (One-Time Password) via SMS
   - Enter the OTP to verify your phone number
   - You'll be prompted to create a new password
   - Complete your profile information if needed

## Required Information

When registering, the following information is needed:

- **First Name**: Your first name
- **Surname**: Your last name
- **Phone Number**: Your mobile phone number (must be in international format, e.g., +254758009278)
- **Country**: Your country of residence
- **Email** (Optional): Your email address if you want to use it as an alternative login method

## Privacy & Security

- Your information is securely transmitted and stored
- SMS verification ensures only you can access your account
- You can update your profile information after logging in
- Your account is created in compliance with Yoma's privacy policy

## Completing Your Profile

After your first login, we recommend:

1. Completing your profile with additional information
2. Setting up security questions for account recovery
3. Exploring opportunities on the platform

## Need Help?

If you encounter any issues:

- **Can't receive OTP**: Make sure your phone number was entered correctly
- **Login problems**: Use the "Forgot Password" option on the Yoma login page
- **Other issues**: Contact Yoma support at support@yoma.world

- Access to opportunities, challenges, and learning resources
- Earn digital credentials and rewards
- Connect with a community of like-minded individuals
